# Overview
This project simulate 3 bodies on space, called Three-Body Dance

### What I use for?
- Rust with Cargo
- Bevy: Game Engine to create visual system


It was beautiful to spend a few days failing and finally see the mathematical combination turn into reality. Understanding the applicable aspects of physics in computing has become my hobby in my spare time. I’ve never been that into math, but seeing it come to life in a visual and applied way almost feels strange, considering our universe is governed by such operational rules. But it also gave meaning to everything I’ve studied so far. It’s nice to revisit concepts I didn’t even realize I had stored away.

I finished the project with that feeling of “I can’t believe what I’m seeing,” like Rapunzel in the movie Tangled when she get out of tower. I made something beautiful while studying, and as long as this feeling stays with me, I’ll know I’m doing what I truly love. Let’s be honest, I could’ve chosen anything else to do during my vacation—doing physical calculations for the position of bodies in space wasn’t even remotely on my list of things to do during a break. Well, I didn’t have a list, but we’ll leave that detail aside.

In the end, I’m happy I pushed through a little more and ended up with this result in my hands.
